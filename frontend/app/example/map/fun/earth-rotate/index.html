Hefei Mars Technology Co., Ltd.
Hefei Mars Technology Co., Ltd. (abbreviation: Mars Technology) was established in Hefei, Anhui in 2017. Since its establishment, the company has been committed to the research and exploration of three-dimensional visualization technology and GIS geographical information technology. The company is engaged in military industry, aerospace, In the fields of simulation, smart city and other industries, the main business model revolves around the Mars2D and Mars3D series of platform-level products, and continues to provide customers with independently controllable, professional and efficient product solutions and services with cutting-edge technology in the visualization industry.
The company has obtained more than 30 software copyrights and patents in the field of three-dimensional visualization. It is a national high-tech enterprise, a "double-soft" certified enterprise, a Hefei big data enterprise, and an AAA certified by the China Software Association. Credit enterprise, has passed ISO9001 quality management system certification.
Mars3D is a WebGL-based software developed by Mars Technology. A 3D client development platform implemented with technology, based on Cesium optimization and B/S architecture design, a lightweight and high-performance GIS development platform that supports multi-industry expansion. It can run efficiently in the browser without installation and plug-ins, and can Quickly access and use a variety of GIS data and 3D models to present three-dimensional space visualization and realize the flexible application of the platform in different industries.
